FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Frax Finance is a DeFi platform focused on stablecoins and liquid staking, combining decentralized infrastructure with products including FRAX (ecosystem token and Fraxtal gas), frxETH (liquid staked ETH), sfrxETH (yield-bearing staked ETH), frxUSD (USD stablecoin backed by tokenized US Treasuries), and Fraxtal L2 network.

FRAX is the main token of the ecosystem — used as the native gas token on Fraxtal L2, for governance over protocol upgrades and parameters, and for utility across all Frax products. It was rebranded from the original FXS token.

frxETH is Frax's liquid staking token for Ethereum. Deposit ETH, receive frxETH which remains liquid for DeFi use. Stake frxETH to get sfrxETH, which earns auto-compounding staking yield from ETH rewards, MEV, and validator tips.

frxUSD is backed primarily by tokenized US Treasuries through WisdomTree's WTGXX vehicle, with a $50M investment commitment from ATW Partners and BitGo custody. This provides institutional-grade, real-world asset backing.

Fraxtal is Frax's own Layer 2 network for efficient, low-cost DeFi transactions. FRAX is the native gas token, and the network natively supports frxETH, frxUSD, and the broader Frax ecosystem. The North Star hardfork completed the transition.

Frax Finance has undergone multiple independent audits, and its smart contracts have operated for several years without major exploits. frxUSD's backing with tokenized US Treasuries and institutional custody via BitGo adds additional trust layers.
